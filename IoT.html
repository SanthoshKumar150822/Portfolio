Smart Level Crossing System

LC gates are automated, when train is detected gates closes automaticaly, if any object stuck betweeen the gates Information is passed to the locomotive pilot and station master to stop the train, as well as to stop any train approaching on the same or opposite side of same track, informed to recovery team, fire-services, police and hospital, if no object is detcted between the gates train leaves the LC and LC gates will open. this system is used to avoid LC accidents and to reduce human power and error.


An Advanced Smart Water Tank Governance System

Water motor is automated motor is turned ON and OFF when water level is LOW and HIGH and user can also operate the motor using voice contol, mobile and web applications. Monitors the turbidity, salinity, and temperature of the water to assess its quality. Status and prediction of water level and other parameters sent to the user as SMS This system avoids the wastage of water, waiting time in terrace, very helpful for senior citizens and disabled persons


Smart Shoe In Health Care

Predicts the walking pattern, fall detection, skeletal disorder, Knee injury, foot ulcer and other foot related diseases. User walks by wearing smart shoe, the force that they applied measured by force resistive sensor, accelerometer for speed and gyroscope for orientation. with these values using ML model able to provide predictions or treatment suggestions regarding the condition of the feet. Medical report with data visualization as charts and graphs send to user mail.


Smart Attendance System using RFID

Maintain records for entry and exit in Schools, Colleges and Industries. Whenever own organization people enters into campus, Library, Labs or Research center. RFID reader reads the signal from RFID tag and UID is uploaded to cloud with current date and time. Usually RFID has default value, we able to write our data in RFID card, for students Rollnumber and for employees employee ID with additional information.


Smart Security System using RFID

Provides security from unauthorized access. In Malls, Banks, Museum, MNC, Government Office, Industry, Universities, Research center and Labs restricted areas are there, to enter into the room, need special access only authorized people have as an UID in RFID, when an user taps the RFID card in the reader if the UID matches in the list of Authorized UIDs then they will get access to enter into the room
